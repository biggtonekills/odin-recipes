Quesadilla

Here is a simple recipe on how to make a quesadilla using three simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Flour Tortillas
  • ham

Instructions

  1. Turn on your stove to medium heat or a 5 if your stove uses numbers.
  2. Place a pan on the stove and wait for it to heat up.
  3. Place a tortilla on the pan, let it heat up to a very light brown color then flip and repeat the process for the other side.
  4. Remove tortilla from heat and add cheese and ham to the tortilla, be careful not to overstuff the tortilla.
  5. Fold your tortilla in half covering the cheese and ham and place it on the pan to heat up again.
  6. Flip the quesadilla until the cheese is melted, dont leave it too long or your tortilla will burn.
  7. Finally remove the quesadilla from the pan, let it sit for about a minute so you don't burn yourself with melted cheese and enjoy.
