Chorizo and Eggs

Here is a simple recipe for chorizo and eggs. You can also add cheese if you're feeling wild.

Igredients

  • 10 oz. Chorizo
  • 3 eggs
  • shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Turn on your stove to medium heat or a 5 if your stove uses numbers.
  2. Place a pan on the stove and wait for it to heat up.
  3. Add in your chorizo and begin to cook until it gets a darker color, be careful noto burn it.
  4. When the chorizo is done, add in your eggs, mix with the already cooked chorizo until the consistency is no longer runny.
  5. Remove from the heat, while the food is still hot add your cheese.
  6. Warm up some flour tortillas and enjoy.
