Agua de jamaica

Here is a simple recipe for Hibiscus iced tea

Igredients

  • 2 quarts of water
  • 3/4 to 1 cup of sugar(depending on how sweet you want it)
  • 1 cup of dried Hibiscus flowers.

Instructions

  1. Put 4 cups of water and the sugar in a medium sauce pan and heat until boiling.
  2. Remove from heat and add in the dried hibiscus flower and stir, cover and let rest for 20 mins.
  3. Strain into a pitcher and throw away the flower.
  4. Add the rest of your water and enjoy.
